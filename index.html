Lounge Guidelines
  • 1All bookings must be submitted through the reservation portal and approved prior to the event.
  • 2Requests should be made at least five days in advance, where possible.
  • 3A safety deposit of $3000 JMD must be given to complete booking. The spot is not secure until the deposit is submitted.
  • 4The individual making the reservation is the primary contact and responsible for the event and its attendees.
  • 5Events must begin and end within the approved reservation period. Overrun will result in forfeiture of 25% of the deposit.
  • 6Organizers must ensure participants are respectful and do not disrupt residents, the office, the athenaeum, or the computer room.
  • 7The maximum occupancy of the lounge must not be exceeded at any time.
  • 8Decorations, displays or equipment may only be installed with prior approval and must not damage lounge property.
  • 9Organizers must restore the lounge to its original condition immediately after the event, including removing all materials.
  • 10Damage, loss or excessive cleaning costs will result in forfeiture of the safety deposit. Full restoration means the deposit is returned.
  • 11Illegal substances, open flames, paint, or spray paint are strictly prohibited.
  • 12Commercial activities, fundraising, or events involving external organizations require prior authorization from hall administration.
  • 13Hall administration reserves the right to cancel any booking that violates these guidelines or poses a safety risk.
  • 14Repeated violations of booking rules may result in suspension of future booking privileges.
